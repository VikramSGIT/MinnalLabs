Privacy Policy

Effective date: 2026-04-21

This policy describes how Minnal Labs ("we", "us") collects and uses information when you use our IoT backend and the devices connected to it.

Information we collect

  • Account information. When you sign in with Google, we receive your Google account's unique identifier, email address, and name.
  • Device data. We store the devices you register, their configuration, firmware version, and state (for example, whether a switch is on or off).
  • Usage data. We log requests to our API and commands sent to your devices to operate the service and diagnose problems.

How we use information

  • To authenticate you and maintain your session.
  • To operate your devices (for example, to deliver an "on" command to a smart plug).
  • To enable integrations you explicitly link, such as Google Home.
  • To maintain, secure, and improve the service.

Third parties

We use the following third parties to provide the service:

  • Google for sign-in and, when you link it, for the Google Home integration.
  • Cloudflare for DNS and network delivery.

We do not sell your personal information.

Data retention

We keep your account and device data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your account and device records; backups are purged on a rolling basis.

Your choices

You can delete your account from within the app at any time, which removes your personal data and unlinks your devices.

Contact

Questions about this policy: privacy@minnallabs.com.

Changes

We may update this policy. Material changes will be posted on this page with a new effective date.
